Top 3 Things I Need for My Hobby

  • Mobile Phone/ Tablet
  • Drawing Software/Application
  • Stylus Pen(to make it better and easier to do)

Steps to Get Started

  1. Download your preferred drawing software/application
  2. Imagine and plan what you want or Search for reference if you want
  3. Use stylus pen to Make a sketch of it
  4. Use your imagination to make it more interesting before finishing your drawing outlines
  5. Add Colors to make it beautiful and lively
